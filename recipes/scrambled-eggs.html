Scrambled Eggs

Description

The best breakfast meal. Easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Olive oil/butter. For the pan.
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Beat the Eggs. Whisk in bowl until yolk and whites are combined.
  2. Preheat the pan. add olive oil or melt butter inside it. Warm over medium heat.
  3. Pour Egg Mixture into the pan. Make sure to stir frequently to prevent egg from drying out.
  4. Continue last step for 3-5 minutes.
