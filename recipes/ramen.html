Ramen Recipe

Description

Also known as the poor university student meal, ive eaten alot of these in my time on this earth.

Ingredients

  • Mr Noodles Instant Ramen
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place Mr Noodles Pack Straight into bowl and boil your water
  2. Once water is boiled, pour into bowl and use fork the push noodles in/break up noodles.
  3. Let noodles sit for a few minutes before emptying the water into the sink
